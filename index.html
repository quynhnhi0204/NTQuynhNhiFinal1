God and Goddess

Wicca is full of metaphors which can co-exist with your current religious or scientific outlook. Because much of Wiccan tradition revolves around Goddess imagery, it has often been considered a woman’s religion. It is not. It is a means of understanding the mysteries of life from a viewpoint which embraces both the feminine “goddess” and masculine “god” energies of the world. You may already think like a Wiccan and not even know it.


Solo Practitioner

The interpretations of rituals and symbols that you find in this guide may be different from those you may find elsewhere. What is important is that you have begun the journey and will find your own right path as a solo practitioner. Trust your innermost intuition and you’ll know what is right for you.


Modern Wisdom

Wicca can transform your life by integrating essential, rural-rooted wisdom into your modern, urban lifestyle. This guide will provide you with knowledge from many sources combined into one practical place. So don’t be afraid… add a little magic in your life!

Spells

white magic

Today, most people consider healing and health spells to be white magic. It is not any more or less difficult than black magic, and even white magic spells can have consequences.

black magic

Real practitioners consider spells which fight against, or try to manipulate free will as black magic. Because these spells try to influence the normal life of others they can become pretty dangerous if they backfire. Free will is considered by some as the strongest force in nature so care should definately be taken when dealing in the dark arts.
